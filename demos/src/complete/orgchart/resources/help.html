Organization Chart

An organizational chart gives a clear picture of a company's staff and structure.

This demo is a great example of how to use many yFiles features with little code to create a powerful application. Observe the benefits of level-of-detail rendering with visually rich node representations: from mere outlines of your data when looking at the entire diagram to all details when zooming in, this technique is easily realized with our library.

Mouse interaction

  • Mouse wheel:
    Changes the zoom level of the view.
  • Left mouse drag:
    Moves the diagram within the view.
  • Left click on employee:
    Selects the employee in both the Chart and the Structure View. Shows the corresponding data in the properties view.
  • Double click:
    Zoom in on the selected employee.
  • Hover over employee:
    Provides tool tip for the employee.
  • Hover over buttons:
    Provides tool tip for actions.
  • Click on buttons:
    Activates corresponding actions to change the visible structure.

Navigation

Zoom to employee
Zoom to current focused employee.
Hide Parent (Page Down)
Hides the parent of the currently-focused employee.
Show Parent (Page Up)
Shows the parent of the currently-focused employee.
Hide Children (Subtract [-])
Hides the children of the currently-focused employee.
Show Children (Add [+])
Shows the children of the currently-focused employee.
All Show All (Multiply [*])
Shows the chart with all employees.

Structure

Mouse interaction

  • Click on employee:
    Selects the employee in both the Organization Chart and the Structure View. Shows the corresponding data in the properties view.
  • Double click on employee:
    Same as Click. Moreover the Organization Chart is zoomed in on the selected employee. If the employee is not currently in the diagram it is set as the new root.
